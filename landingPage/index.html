Locally deployed AI model

Analyse your emotion changes using highly accurate, locally deployed AI model.

A face expression recognition model trained using the lightweight AI model ShuffleNet 2.0 tracks changes in your expressions at work and translates them into a record of your happiness index in the database.

There is no need to worry about leakage of the data captured by the camera as it is analysed locally and is automatically deleted when the analysis is complete. Your privacy always comes first!

Track your data changes

Without any action, the record of your focus time is analysed automatically!

HappyTomato automatically sorts through your database of focus times and calculates your total focus time as well as your average focus time.

You can arrange your focus time more rationally based on the results of these data analyses!

Detailed history

All your focus time is saved!

On the history page, there will be a record of all the focused time you have spent using HappyTomato since you started. This includes the time you started focusing, the duration of your focus, and your happiness index.

The icon on the side of the happiness index will also change automatically according to the value of the happiness index, and the result can be seen at a glance!

Highly customisable

HappyTomato is yours, feel free to customise it!

There is a high level of customisation in HappyTomato, with the user being able to choose whether or not to turn on data analysis as well as emotion analysis.

On top of that, the app has a built-in dark theme and the ability to follow the system to switch themes, making HappyTomato even more in tune with your preferences!
